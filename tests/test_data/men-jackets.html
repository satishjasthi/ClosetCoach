        Men’s Jackets are one of those underrated pieces of apparel that are often overlooked. Besides keeping you warm or allowing you to gradually cool off, they add depth and definition to every attire you wear. Is it cold out?         Types of Men’s Jackets You Can Buy

        Men’s jackets have been part of the major fashion overhaul over the last decade as people are finding out how truly versatile jackets are. You can find men's jacket prices ranging from as low as Rs. 449 to as high as Rs. 55,000 on Myntra. Before you decide to buy men's jackets, take a look at the different types available.

      • Bomber Jackets

        Bomber jackets first appeared in the 1950s when the US army commissioned nylon men jackets that were durable, water-resistant, and lightweight. Ever since, every generation and subculture has borrowed the bomber, thus spawning a few dozen trendy variations. You can pair a bomber jacket with any branded jeans for men, a T-shirt, and a pair of cool sneakers, to complete a classic all-weather look.

      • Polo Jackets

        Polo jackets get their name from the horseback field sport popularised by the British aristocracy. These jackets, originally known as polo men coats, are long, beige jackets players would wear post-game. You can find two distinct types of polo jackets -- the classic trench style with fabric belt fastener and the double-breast style jacket with button fastener. 

      • Puffer Jackets

        Down jackets, popularised as puffer jackets by the fashion industry, are quilted men jackets that are designed for the winter. They are well-insulated to provide sufficient resistance against wind, rain, and cold, and to keep you warm at all times. So, if you’re in search of mens puffer jacket for winter, you can go right ahead and shop from hundreds of unique and exclusive products on Myntra.

      • Tailored Jackets

        If you’re looking for men's jackets for party wear, the tailored variants are what you should be looking at. These are basically blazers which add a touch of elegance and sophistication when paired with a shirt or a t-shirt, a pair of trousers, and a pair of moccasins. A classic rule of thumb when wearing men's jackets like these is to wear them with contrasting trousers. For instance, if your tailored jacket is light in colour, pair them with navy blue or black trousers and vice versa.
