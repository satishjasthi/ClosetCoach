        Sunglasses for men have become an iconic accessory and moved beyond just being functional protection for the eyes. When it comes to wearing sunglasses, they give a suave, classy touch to men’s faces, giving them a cool aura.

        Such is their impact that they have been a part of many iconic looks in Hollywood and Bollywood movies which went on to become a rage among men. Apart from this, hip-hop, rock'n' roll and rap artists were also instrumental in shaping the styles of sunglasses for men around the world. Myntra has an amazing collection of men's sunglasses that can set a killer impression.

        HANDLE WITH CARE - SUNGLASSES FOR MEN

        If you wish these sunglasses to look cool for longer, we have some handy tips and tricks that can help you with that.

        • Ensure that you rinse your sunglasses in water before wiping it. This is to prevent them from getting scratches from dust particles, which happens when you dry wipe them. It is recommended that you use a special spray to clean them and not household cleaning liquids. After this, leave them to air dry or use only a lint-free fabric to dry off.
        • Regardless of how soft material, it can damage your sunglasses. The damage is not visible initially but adds up over time. The cloth you use can also transfer dirt onto the lenses so be mindful. For this reason, you should reserve a cloth to clean your sunglasses.
        • Never place your sunglasses with the lenses facing down and don't touch the lenses with your bare fingers to keep prints at bay.
